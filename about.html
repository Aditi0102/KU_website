Project


Our Project is built with an aim to provide safety and security to all resedential areas and public areas. A real-time intelligent surveillance system was developed for home security with a vision to prevent all the assualt and mishappenings in and around the resedential areas and get immediate alerts of any breach of privacy and safety directly in your cell phones.

The envisioned goal is achieved by first capturing the frames and training the model and in real-time detecting if the activity can be termed as suspicious or not. If the output comes out to be suspicious, the system immedialtely sends a notification to the concerned authority alerting them of the crime taking place. And an immediate action can be taken to prevent any rampage taking place.
